Who We Are

At Kayangana Eco-Palms, we are building a modern palm-processing company that connects traditional farming with practical innovation. Our focus is to improve oil extraction, strengthen livelihoods, and protect the environment through smart mechanization.

Founded by a team trained in ethical leadership at Ashesi University, we believe business can be a force for measurable social change. We are building more than a factory — we are building an ecosystem of empowerment for the Rumonge community.

  • Mechanized, high-quality processing that meets international standards
  • Community-centered service model that empowers farmers
  • Zero-waste circular economy protecting our environment

How We Create Value

Three practical pillars that shape how we operate

⚙️

Mechanized, High-Quality Processing

We replace grueling manual extraction with efficient, safe processing that delivers branded virgin palm oil below the 10.0 mg KOH/g acidity limit.

🤝

Community-Centered Service

We empower farmers through affordable access to modern equipment, reducing processing time and helping them maximize yield.

♻️

Zero-Waste Environmental Protection

We turn palm byproducts into eco-friendly charcoal and organic fertilizer, creating value while protecting the local environment.

Long-term Impact

Our work goes beyond refining palm oil. In the long term, a share of our profits will support the Kayangana Foundation and broader community impact initiatives.

  • Fund local scholarships to keep children in school
  • Provide youth leadership training programs
  • Build community centers with reliable internet access
  • Connect rural talent with global opportunities
