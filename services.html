THE SMART HUB MODEL

Four key differentiators that set Kayangana Eco-Palms apart

01

Health & Quality Assurance

We replace the grueling 5-to-7 day manual fermentation process with rapid, mechanized extraction. Our oil stays well below the 10.0 mg KOH/g international acidity limit — a standard local artisanal producers currently fail to meet.

02

Zero-Waste Circular Economy

We transform agricultural residues — leftover palm fibers and shells — into eco-friendly charcoal and nutrient-rich organic fertilizer, creating additional revenue while protecting the environment.

03

100% Renewable Energy

We are committed to running our operations entirely on solar power, moving away from the massive deforestation caused by traditional wood-fired boiling methods.

04

Ethical Dual-Revenue Model

Farmers can either sell fresh fruit directly at a fair price, or pay a small fee to use our efficient equipment. They are partners in our growth, not just suppliers.

THE SOLUTION IN ACTION

Our proposed processing hub and product line

Motorized Oil Expellers

High-efficiency mechanical expellers replace manual extraction, dramatically increasing yield and reducing processing time from days to hours.

Filtration & Branded Packaging

Processed oil is filtered and packaged under the Kayangana brand, ensuring consistent quality and access to formal markets including supermarkets and restaurants in Bujumbura.

Eco-Charcoal & Organic Fertilizer

Palm fiber and shell residues are converted into eco-charcoal and organic fertilizer — creating additional revenue streams and eliminating agricultural waste.

Solar Power Infrastructure

Our hub runs entirely on renewable solar energy, eliminating dependence on wood fuel and protecting Rumonge's forests from deforestation.

Direct Farmer Partnerships

We purchase fresh palm fruit directly from smallholder farmers at fair, transparent prices — eliminating exploitative middlemen and ensuring stable farmer incomes.

Community Service Hub

Farmers who prefer to process their own fruit can pay a small service fee to use our equipment — maximizing their efficiency and yield while keeping them in control.

Funding Our Pilot Smart Hub

Our pilot Smart Hub in Rumonge requires a total initial capital injection of $5,000. Through our placement in the ACE Incubator at Ashesi University, we have already secured 30% ($1,500) of this required capital.

Grant funding will be strategically allocated to close our remaining 70% funding gap — directed entirely toward Capital Expenditures to operationalize the site:

  • Procurement and installation of motorized oil expellers
  • Filtration units for quality-compliant oil production
  • Initial solar power system infrastructure
  • Site preparation and operational setup in Rumonge
